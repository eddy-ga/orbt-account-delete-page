3. What will be removed

Once your request is processed, the following information is permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Account profile and authentication data
  • Saved topics, preferences, and personalization settings
  • All in-app usage data tied to your account
Important

Deleted data cannot be restored. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Orbt again in the future.
